Debut Album from Delta Deep

Def Leppard guitarist Phil Collen has released a new album with his blues-based music project, Delta Deep. Their debut album features a mix of original and hand-picked cover tracks, but stylistically it is not confined to any one genre.

Collen enlisted the help of Stone Temple Pilots' bass player Robert DeLeo, singer Debbi Blackwell-Cook (backup vocalist for such artists as Michael Buble and Luther Vandross) and drummer Forrest Robinson (drummer for India.Arie, Joe Sample and the Crusaders, TLC) to create his new blues-, funk- and soul-flavored band.
